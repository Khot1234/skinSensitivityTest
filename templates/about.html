About Edwin Kriti Derma Solutions

Edwin Kriti Derma Solutions is dedicated to helping you better understand your skin. Our simple, friendly tools aim to raise awareness about skin sensitivity and support informed skincare choices.

This website offers two approaches: a quick questionnaire based on common sensitivity indicators and an optional image analysis that uses basic visual metrics. Results are for educational purposes and are not a substitute for professional medical advice.

Our Mission

Make skincare insights accessible with modern, privacy-conscious tools and a delightful user experience.
